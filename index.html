The 4 Pillars of Democracy

📝

Voter Registration

The first step to making your voice heard.

Citizens must register to vote to prove eligibility. This involves providing identification and proof of residence to local election officials.

📢

Campaigning

How candidates share their vision.

Candidates use debates, rallies, and advertisements to inform the public about their policies and earn trust before election day.

🗳️

Voting

The moment of decision.

On election day, voters go to polling stations or use mail-in ballots to cast their secret vote for their preferred candidate.

📊

Counting

Ensuring every vote matters.

Ballots are securely collected and tallied. Official results are certified only after rigorous verification processes.
